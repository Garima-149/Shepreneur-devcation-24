Frequently Asked Questions

What is Shepreneur and what is its mission?

Shepreneur is dedicated to providing a supportive platform for women to thrive as entrepreneurs. Our mission is to empower women entrepreneurs through technology, investment, and ensure holistic well-being for sustainable business growth and innovation.

How does Shepreneur support women in entrepreneurship?

Shepreneur supports women in entrepreneurship through various means such as providing a platform to sell their products, connecting them with women in tech for business development, facilitating investment opportunities, and offering stress-relieving activities and meditation resources.

Who are the women in tech and how can they help improve my business?

Women in tech are professional women skilled in website development, app development, and other technical areas. They can help promote your business by creating and optimizing your online presence, developing customized solutions, and providing technical support.

How can I apply for investment through Shepreneur?

To apply for investment through Shepreneur, submit your business proposal detailing your project, goals, and financial needs. Our team will review your proposal and connect you with potential investors if it aligns with their requirements.

How can I get in touch with Shepreneur for further inquiries or support?

You can contact Shepreneur by filling out the contact form on our website or sending an email to our customer support team at contact@shepreneur.com . We strive to respond to all inquiries promptly and provide the necessary assistance.
